HISTORY

The Orthodox church is the ancient christian church that traces it foundation back to our Lord Jesus Christ and His Tweleve Apostles. The church has continued has pereserved the apostolic doctrine and tradition, worship, and structure. The orthodox church maintains that the church is the living Body of Jesus Christ.
