HISTORY

The Orthodox church is the ancient christian church that traces it foundation back to our Lord Jesus Christ and His Tweleve Apostles. The church has continued has pereserved the apostolic doctrine and tradition, worship, and structure. The orthodox church maintains that the church is the living Body of Jesus Christ.

The Ethiopian Tewahedo Orthodox Church is among the Oriental Orthodox churches which are referred to as "Non-Chalcedonian", and, sometimes by outsiders as "monophysite" (meaning "One Single Nature", in reference to Christ). The Ethiopian Church claims its earliest origins from the royal official said to have been baptized by Philip the Evangelist (Acts 8:27).
